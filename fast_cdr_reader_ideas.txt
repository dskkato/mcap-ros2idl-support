Ideas for optimizing CdrReader:

- Implement a FastCdrReader subclass that caches ``struct.Struct`` instances for
  each primitive type to avoid repeatedly parsing format strings during
  decoding.
- Replace ``rosmsg2_serialization``'s ``CdrReader`` with ``FastCdrReader`` to
  reduce overhead when reading many small fields.
- Run benchmarks comparing the stock reader and the optimized version to
  quantify performance improvements.
- Consider contributing the optimization directly to the ``cdr`` package so all
  consumers benefit.
